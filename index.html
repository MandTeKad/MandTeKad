About the Author

MandTeKad writes dark self-insert fanfiction — stories where the protagonist enters a broken world already knowing how it ends, and chooses to rewrite it anyway.

No chosen-one tropes. No clean victories. Just the slow grind of building something real in a world that wants to kill you for trying.

530,000+ words across Star Wars, ASOIAF, Harry Potter, Skyrim, Danny Phantom, and Warhammer 40,000.
