The Gospel of Swordsmanship

A field manual for the solo practitioner. A stick and a dream.

Six years of active practice. The Gospel of Swordsmanship was originally built for my own practice and the training of a student — a synthetic system informed by historical sources, built for people who don't have a club, a partner, or perfect conditions.
